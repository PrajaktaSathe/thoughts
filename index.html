Hello and Welcome!!!


I am Prajakta Sathe, an engineering student from India. Being interested in web development and writing both, I was tempted to create a simple website which would portray my writing skills along with coding skills. So, as you would see, the website is still in the developmental stages.


My passion for writing and my readers' interest in reading my literary works is half of my inspiration for this website. The other half is, ofcourse my attraction towards programming. I had always wanted to create a website of my own, that too from scratch (it was a long lost dream, until now!). Accordingly, I decided to do something fruitful, and to fulfill my childhood dream, taking advantage of this ongoing lockdown. So I took up this challenge, and here is the result of my challenge. I would not say, just yet, that this website is fully complete, but, I will definitely say it is am outcome of my hardwork of many days. This is also giving me a platform to portray my writing skills (as you would not find me anywhere on social media!), similar to a blog!


The process was what was more important to me, personally. I learned a lot of things while coding up a website. I wouldn't say that developing this website was a total cakewalk. I stumbled upon many things. I had to sort out a lot of things. I had to do many trial and errors. I had to tackle many other technical problems, along with managing the aesthetics of the project as well. But, all-in-all, it was quite an humbling experience, and I enjoyed it alot!! I hope you also enjoy reading and navigating on the website!


Feel free to explore around!!
