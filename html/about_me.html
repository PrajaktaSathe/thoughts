Hi!

I am Prajakta, from Pune, India. I am currently a student of engineering. I am passionate about a variety of things, including trying to learn new things. I am fond of writing in English and Marathi (my mother-tongue), along with reading novels and books (I am a huge fan of Harry Potter!!). I also include sketching, drawing, painting, quilling, and other forms of visual arts in my list of hobbies. Along with all the artistic things mentioned above, I am more of a 'nature' person. Trekking is a thing I absolutely like. I like being in the midst of nature, as it provides a way to explore nature's secrets, and also provides a break from the daily chaotic life.


If there is one thing I would like to do in my life, it is to travel the world, visit all the 7 continents, and explore the cultures of various countries around the world.


I am an optimistic person, and, being an optimistic, I try to find positivity in even the worst of times. I try to seek happiness in smallest of things.
